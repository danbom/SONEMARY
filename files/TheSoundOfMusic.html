The Sound Of Music

Romance, Drama, Musical | English | 1978
DirectorRobert Wise
Release date1978-02-04
CountryUSA
Running time2h 52m
Mariah, a music-loving tomboy nun, becomes a tutor for a well-known family member of the Navy's Von Trap family at the recommendation of the head nun. "Maria" gradually communicates with the seven children of the Von Trap family, who rarely open their hearts, and the strict "Phone Trap Colonel" also opens their hearts. Then one day, Maria realizes she loves Colonel Phone Trap and decides to leave her children's side and go back to the convent with her own secrets.
